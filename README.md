## 🧑‍💻 About me

I'm a developer who enjoys poking at systems from the bottom up. My interests sit at the intersection of **web development**, **system administration**, and **cryptography** — three fields that share the same itch: *how does this actually work under the hood?*

- 🦀 **Currently learning Rust** and enjoying every bit of the borrow checker.
- 🐘 Comfortable shipping server-side code with **PHP**.
- 🐍 Occasional **Python** contributor on projects coming out of **Kaleido**.
- 🔐 Reading up on modern cryptography primitives and their real-world pitfalls.
- 🛠️ Happiest in a Linux terminal.
